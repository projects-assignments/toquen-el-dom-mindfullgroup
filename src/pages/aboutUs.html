MUSIC SCHOOL KIDS

MUSIC SCHOOL KIDS is a space where exceptional talent can thrive by encouraging artists to stretch, experiment, improvise, and play. Our school provides artists with the tools they need to become not just great musicians but great leaders. Our team of teachers is made up of active professional musicians with international projection, higher education and an extensive and consolidated career in their instrument. They have a high degree of pedagogical quality, thanks to a demanding preparation process.

MAVA Drum-Kit, JMC & Musical Coordinator

ESP / ENG

Her passion for music leads her to study “serious” in modern and classical music schools in the north of her country of origin. Later, she studied jazz drums in Spain at Berklee and at the Liceo Superior Conservatory of Music.She has collaborated with numerous artists in Germany, Portugal and Spain, with musical projects of very varied styles: jazz, big band, funk, fusion, blues, rockabilly, rock, post rock, progressive rock, Brazilian music, indie, electronic and pop.
